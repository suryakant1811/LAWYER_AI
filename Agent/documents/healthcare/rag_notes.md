# Healthcare RAG Notes


- Identify whether dispute is medical, regulatory, or privacy related.

- Check Consumer Protection Act for compensation claims.

- Check BNS Section 106 only for gross negligence.

- Medical adverse outcome does not automatically mean negligence.

- Verify expert opinion requirement.

- Check DPDP Act for healthcare data issues.

- Check CDSCO rules for drugs and devices.

- Verify latest healthcare notifications.
