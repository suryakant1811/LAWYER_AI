# Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA)

Protects personal data.

Applicable in:

- Data Breaches
- Unauthorized data sharing
- Privacy violations

---

# Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA)

Used for:

- Investment scams
- Large cyber frauds
- Mule accounts

Authorities may freeze accounts.

---

# POCSO Act

Applicable where cyber offences involve children.

Examples:

- Child sexual abuse material
- Online grooming
- Exploitation of minors
