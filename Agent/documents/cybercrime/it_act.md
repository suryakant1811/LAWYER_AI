# Information Technology Act, 2000

Primary legislation governing cybercrime in India.

## Section 43

Civil liability for unauthorized access, virus attacks, data theft and system damage.

---

## Section 65

Tampering with Computer Source Code

Punishment:
- Up to 3 years imprisonment
- Fine up to ₹2 lakh

---

## Section 66

Computer related offences committed dishonestly or fraudulently.

Punishment:
- Up to 3 years imprisonment
- Fine up to ₹5 lakh

---

## Section 66A

Declared unconstitutional in:

Shreya Singhal v Union of India (2015)

Cannot be used anymore.

---

## Section 66B

Receiving stolen computer resources.

---

## Section 66C

Identity Theft

Includes:

- OTP theft
- Password theft
- Aadhaar misuse
- Digital Signature misuse

Punishment:

Up to 3 years imprisonment + ₹1 lakh fine.

---

## Section 66D

Cheating by Personation

Examples:

- Phishing
- Fake bank calls
- Fake UPI requests
- Fake KYC
- Online scams

Punishment:

Up to 3 years imprisonment + ₹1 lakh fine.

---

## Section 66E

Violation of Privacy

Sharing private images without consent.

---

## Section 66F

Cyber Terrorism

Punishment:

Life imprisonment.
