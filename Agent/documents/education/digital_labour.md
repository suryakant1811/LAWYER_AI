# DIGITALISATION & CYBER LAW

## Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), 2023

Recognises:

- Cybercrime
- Electronic communication
- Organised crime

Electronic communications may constitute evidence for offences involving:

- Defamation
- Extortion
- Threats
- Hate speech

---

## Extra-Territorial Abetment

A person outside India may be prosecuted for abetting offences committed within India through electronic means.

---

## Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (BSA), 2023

Electronic evidence includes:

- Emails
- WhatsApp chats
- Screenshots
- PDFs
- Digital logs

Electronic evidence is primary evidence.

Authenticity requirements must be satisfied.

Certificate requirements continue where applicable.

---

## Digital Proceedings

Courts may:

- Conduct proceedings electronically
- Direct production of electronic devices
- Admit digital documents
