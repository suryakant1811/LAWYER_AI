# LABOUR CODES

## Four Labour Codes

India has consolidated 29 labour laws into four Labour Codes.

Implementation is expected gradually across States.

---

## Code on Wages, 2019

Consolidates laws relating to:

- Minimum wages
- Timely payment of wages
- Bonus
- Equal remuneration

---

## Code on Social Security, 2020

Covers:

- EPF
- ESI
- Gratuity
- Maternity benefit
- Gig workers
- Platform workers
- Unorganized workers

---

## Industrial Relations Code, 2020

Deals with:

- Trade unions
- Standing orders
- Retrenchment
- Lay-off
- Industrial disputes
- Strikes
- Lock-outs

---

## Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code, 2020

Provides rules regarding:

- Safety
- Health
- Welfare
- Appointment letters
- Working conditions
- Working hours
