# GLOSSARY

## Workman

Employee performing:

- Manual work
- Skilled work
- Technical work
- Clerical work
- Supervisory work

and not primarily managerial.

---

## Non-Workman

Managerial or administrative employee governed mainly through contract.

---

## Retrenchment

Termination otherwise than disciplinary dismissal.

---

## Domestic Enquiry

Internal disciplinary proceeding conducted before dismissal.

---

## Organised Crime

Includes cybercrime under BNS when committed by an organised group.

---

## Electronic Communication

Digital communication including:

- Email
- SMS
- Messaging apps
- Video
- Images

---

## Provident Fund

Retirement savings scheme with employer and employee contributions.

---

## Overtime

Work beyond statutory hours compensated at twice the ordinary rate.
