# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Zero FIR?

Zero FIR allows filing FIR at any police station regardless of location.

---

## Can police arrest without warrant?

Yes, for cognisable offences.

---

## How long can police custody continue under BNSS?

Depending on offence:

60 or 90 days maximum with judicial approval.

---

## Can police refuse FIR due to jurisdiction?

No.

Zero FIR mechanism allows registration anywhere.

---

## What is preliminary enquiry under BNSS?

For certain offences between 3-7 years punishment, police may conduct enquiry within 14 days after DSP approval.
