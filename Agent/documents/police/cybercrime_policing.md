# Cybercrime Policing India

## E-Zero FIR Pilot

Started:

May 2025

Purpose:

Automatic FIR conversion for major cyber financial crimes.

---

## Features

- Integration with NCRB CCTNS
- Faster FIR registration
- Evidence preservation
- Victim tracking

---

# Zero FIR

Zero FIR allows registration:

- At any police station
- Regardless of jurisdiction

Later transferred to competent police station.

---

# IT Act Investigation

Section 78 IT Act:

Investigation officer must be:

Inspector rank or above.

# Cyber Crime Reporting

Victims can report through:

- National Cybercrime Portal
- 1930 Cyber Helpline
