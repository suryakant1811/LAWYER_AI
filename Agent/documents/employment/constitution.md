# Constitutional Framework of Employment Law — India

## Articles 14, 15, 16 — Equality and Non-Discrimination

The Constitution prohibits discrimination in employment on grounds of religion, race, caste, sex, or place of birth.

Article 16 guarantees equality of opportunity in public employment.

Labour Codes prohibit gender-based discrimination including against transgender persons.

---

## Article 21 — Right to Life and Personal Liberty

In employment context Article 21 includes:

- Safe working environment
- Fair treatment
- Protection from sexual harassment
- Dignity at workplace

---

## Directive Principles of State Policy

These guide the State in ensuring:

- Fair wages
- Humane working conditions
- Social security
