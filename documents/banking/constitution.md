# Constitutional Framework of Banking Law

## Article 19(1)(g)
Right to practice any profession or carry on trade or business.
Banking companies operate under this right subject to reasonable restrictions under Article 19(6).

Important:
- RBI licensing
- Banking Regulation Act
- Public interest
- Depositor protection

---

## Article 21

Right to Life.

Relevant in banking for:

- Illegal account freezing
- Harassment by recovery agents
- Coercive recovery
- Due process

Supreme Court has repeatedly held that recovery must respect dignity.

---

## Article 300A

Right to Property.

Relevant in

- Bank account freezing
- Property attachment
- SARFAESI auction
- Deposit seizure

No deprivation except authority of law.

---

## Articles 32 & 226

Constitutional Remedies.

Used against

- Illegal RBI actions
- Arbitrary circulars
- Wrongful freezing
- Denial of banking services

---

## Article 14

Equality before law.

Invoked in

- Discriminatory loan sanctions
- Unequal RBI implementation
- Arbitrary treatment

---
