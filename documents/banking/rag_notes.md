# RAG Notes

When answering:

- Differentiate Civil vs Criminal remedies.

- Verify latest RBI Circulars.

- Check SARFAESI timelines.

- Verify KYC compliance.

- Cross-reference FEMA for NRI issues.

- Cross-reference Consumer Protection for service deficiency.

- Use RBI Master Directions wherever applicable.

- Mention DRT remedies before High Court if applicable.

- Mention Ombudsman before litigation for customer grievances.

- Verify monetary thresholds against latest RBI notifications.
