# Glossary

Intermediary

Platform that stores or transmits information.

Examples:

- WhatsApp
- Facebook
- Instagram
- Amazon

---

Identity Theft

Unauthorized use of:

- Password
- OTP
- Aadhaar
- Electronic Signature

---

Digital Arrest

Fraud where scammers pretend to be police or government officials.

---

Zero FIR

FIR that can be filed at any police station regardless of jurisdiction.

---

Mule Account

Bank account used to transfer stolen money.

---

Safe Harbour

Protection available to intermediaries under Section 79.
