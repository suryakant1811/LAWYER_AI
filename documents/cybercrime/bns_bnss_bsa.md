# Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS)

Applicable alongside the IT Act.

Common sections used with cybercrime:

- Cheating
- Forgery
- Criminal Intimidation
- Defamation

Cyber fraud FIRs usually invoke:

- IT Act 66C
- IT Act 66D
- BNS Cheating provisions

---

# Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS)

Procedural law.

Provides:

- FIR
- Zero FIR
- Investigation
- Arrest
- Search
- Seizure

---

# Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (BSA)

Electronic Evidence Law.

Electronic evidence includes:

- Screenshots
- WhatsApp Chats
- Emails
- Call Records
- CCTV
- Transaction Records

Secondary electronic evidence generally requires certification.
