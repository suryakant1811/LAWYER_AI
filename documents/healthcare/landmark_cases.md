# Healthcare Landmark Cases


## Jacob Mathew v State of Punjab (2005)


Issue:

Medical negligence standard.


Principle:

Criminal medical negligence requires:

- Expert medical opinion
- Deviation from accepted practice
- Causal connection


## Res Ipsa Loquitur Principle


Meaning:

Thing speaks for itself.


Applied where negligence is obvious.


Examples:

- Surgical instruments left inside body
