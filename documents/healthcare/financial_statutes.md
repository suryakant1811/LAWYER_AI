# Healthcare Related Statutes


## Consumer Protection Act 2019


Medical negligence compensation route.


Deficiency of service includes:

- Fault
- Shortcoming
- Poor quality service


Limitation:

2 years


Consumer Commission:

District

State

National


## BNS Section 106

Death caused by rash/negligent act.

Applicable for gross medical negligence.


## Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023


Applies to:

- Hospitals
- Labs
- Health apps
- Telemedicine platforms


Key requirements:

- Consent
- Security safeguards
- Breach reporting


Penalties:

₹250 crore

Security failure


₹200 crore

Failure to notify breach
