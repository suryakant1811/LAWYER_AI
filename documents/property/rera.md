# Real Estate Regulation and Development Act, 2016 (RERA)

## Purpose

RERA regulates real estate sector.

Objectives:

- Protect homebuyers
- Increase transparency
- Prevent fund diversion
- Ensure timely possession


---

# Section 3 — Project Registration

Promoters cannot:

- Advertise
- Sell
- Market

without RERA registration.


---

# Section 13 — Escrow Account

Promoters must deposit:

70% of project funds

Purpose:

Only construction and land expenses.


---

# Agreement For Sale

Required before accepting more than 10% payment from buyer.


---

# Penalties

Failure to comply:

- Imprisonment up to 3 years
- Fine up to 10% project cost


---

# Challenges

- Stalled projects
- Delayed possession
- Fund diversion
- Non-compliance with orders
