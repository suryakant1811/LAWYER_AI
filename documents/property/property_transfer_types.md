# Property Transfer Types

## Gift

Transfer without consideration.

Effective:

Immediately after acceptance.


---

## Settlement

Transfer usually among family members.

Characteristics:

- Love and affection
- Immediate transfer
- Cannot generally be revoked


---

## Will

Testamentary document.

Characteristics:

- Effective after death
- Revocable anytime before death


---

# Supreme Court Principle

The nature of document depends on:

- Intention
- Substance
- Rights created

Not merely title of document.
