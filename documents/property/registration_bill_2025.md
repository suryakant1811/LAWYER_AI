# Registration Bill 2025

The proposed Registration Bill aims to replace Registration Act 1908.

Objectives:

- Digital registration
- Paperless process
- Transparency


---

# Major Changes

## Mandatory Registration Expansion

Includes:

- Sale agreements
- Property transfer Power of Attorney
- Sale certificates
- Equitable mortgage documents
- Court orders affecting property


---

# Online Registration

Features:

- Electronic submission
- Digital certificates
- Online records


---

# Aadhaar Authentication

Allowed with:

- Consent
- Alternative verification options


---

# Equitable Mortgage

Banks must notify registrar regarding mortgage creation.

---

# Appeals

Structured appeal system introduced.

Reduces court litigation.
