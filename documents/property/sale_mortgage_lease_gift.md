# Property Transactions Explained

## Sale

Transfer of complete ownership against monetary consideration.

Section:

54 Transfer of Property Act


---

# Mortgage

Security created over property for repayment of loan.

## Mortgagor

Person giving security.

## Mortgagee

Person receiving security.


---

# Lease

Creates right to use and enjoy property.

Lease creates interest.

License only gives permission.


---

# Gift

Transfer without consideration.

Gift becomes effective after acceptance.

---

# Gift vs Settlement vs Will

## Gift

- Immediate transfer
- No consideration
- Voluntary


## Settlement

- Usually between family members
- Based on love and affection
- Immediate ownership transfer
- Generally irrevocable


## Will

- Operates after death
- Revocable during lifetime
