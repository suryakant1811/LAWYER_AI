# Landmark Property Law Cases

## Property Owners Association v State of Maharashtra (2024)

Held:

Private property cannot automatically be considered community resources under Article 39(b).


---

## Associated Hotels of India Ltd v R.N Kapoor (1959)

Held:

Difference between lease and licence depends on intention of parties.


---

## Gift Settlement Will Judgment (2025)

Supreme Court clarified:

Substance of document is more important than its title.

Settlement and gift create immediate rights.

Will operates after death.
