# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can government take private property?

Only through authority of law.

Supreme Court protected private property rights in 2024.


---

## Is agreement to sell ownership proof?

No.

Ownership transfers only after registered sale deed.


---

## What is equitable mortgage?

Mortgage created by depositing title documents with lender.


---

## Can settlement deed be cancelled?

Generally no, if executed voluntarily with transfer of ownership.


---

## Does RERA protect buyers?

Yes.

It regulates builders and protects homebuyers.


---

## Is gift deed immediate?

Yes, after acceptance by donee.
