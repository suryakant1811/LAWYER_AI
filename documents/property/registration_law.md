# Registration Act, 1908

Registration Act governs registration of property documents.

Purpose:

- Maintain property records
- Prevent fraud
- Provide legal validity

---

# Section 17 — Compulsory Registration

Documents requiring registration:

- Gift deeds
- Property transfer documents
- Long-term leases
- Documents creating interest in property

---

# Agreements For Sale

Currently:

Registration required when possession is transferred under Section 53A ToPA.

Without possession:

Generally not compulsory.

---

# Importance

Registered documents provide:

- Legal evidence
- Ownership proof
- Protection against disputes
