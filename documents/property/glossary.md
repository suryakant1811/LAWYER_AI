# Property Law Glossary

## Sale

Transfer of ownership for money consideration.

## Mortgage

Security interest created over property.

## Lease

Right to enjoy property for fixed period.

## Gift

Voluntary transfer without consideration.

## Settlement

Family property transfer based on affection.

## Will

Declaration effective after death.

## Equitable Mortgage

Mortgage created by deposit of title deeds.

## RERA

Real Estate Regulation and Development Act.

## Article 300A

Constitutional protection of property rights.
