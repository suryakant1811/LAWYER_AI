# LANDMARK CASES

## Air India v. Nargesh Meerza

Dismissal based on pregnancy or marriage was held unconstitutional.

---

## Central Inland Water Transport Corporation Ltd. v. Brojo Nath Ganguly

Unconscionable employment contract clauses may be struck down.

---

## Niranjan Shankar Golikari v. Century Spinning

Reasonable restraints during employment are enforceable.

---

## Superintendence Company of India v. Krishan Murgai

Post-employment non-compete clauses are generally void.

---

## Vishaka v. State of Rajasthan

Laid down sexual harassment guidelines that ultimately resulted in the POSH Act.

---

## Indian Medical Association cases and labour jurisprudence

Electronic evidence and procedural fairness continue evolving under BSA and Labour Codes.
