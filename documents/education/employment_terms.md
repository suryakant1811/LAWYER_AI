# EMPLOYMENT TERMS & CONDITIONS

## Appointment Letter

Employers are generally expected to issue appointment letters.

Under the OSH Code every employee will receive an appointment letter containing prescribed particulars.

---

## Employment Contract

Recommended clauses include:

- Salary
- Designation
- Duties
- Confidentiality
- Notice period
- Leave
- Working hours
- Termination
- Intellectual Property
- Non-disclosure

---

## Working Hours

Standard working hours:

- 9 hours/day
- 48 hours/week

Overtime is payable at:

- Twice the ordinary wage

---

## Women Working at Night

Many States permit night shifts subject to:

- Safe transport
- Security
- CCTV
- Women supervisors
- Emergency support

---

## Leave

Employees generally receive:

- Casual leave
- Sick leave
- Earned leave
- National holidays

Earned leave eligibility generally arises after:

- 240 working days

Under the OSH Code:

- Proposed reduction to 180 days
- Leave encashment provisions

---

## Social Security

### Provident Fund

Employer contribution:

- 12%

Employee contribution:

- 12%

Applicable to covered establishments.

International workers are also covered subject to Social Security Agreements.

---

## Maternity Leave

Eligibility:

- 80 days service in previous 12 months

Benefit:

- 26 weeks paid leave

---

## Paternity Leave

No central statutory provision.

Many employers voluntarily provide:

- 5–7 days
