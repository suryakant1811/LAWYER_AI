# CONSTITUTIONAL & STATUTORY FRAMEWORK

## Constitutional Basis

### Article 14 — Equality before Law
Prohibits discrimination in employment on grounds such as sex, religion, caste, etc.

### Article 15 — Prohibition of Discrimination
Empowers the State to make special provisions for women, children, and socially and educationally backward classes.

The Supreme Court has reaffirmed that dismissal of a female employee solely because of marital status amounts to unconstitutional gender discrimination.

### Article 16 — Equality of Opportunity in Public Employment
Guarantees equal opportunity for all citizens in matters relating to employment or appointment under the State.

### Article 21 — Right to Life and Personal Liberty
Includes:

- Right to livelihood
- Safe working environment
- Fair treatment
- Protection against workplace harassment
- Dignity at work

## Key Supporting Legislations

### Industrial Disputes Act, 1947

Governs:

- Retrenchment
- Industrial disputes
- Lay-off
- Closure
- Reinstatement
- Compensation

### Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013

The definition of workplace includes:

- Office
- Branch office
- Client location
- Transportation
- Work from home
- Residence

### Maternity Benefit Act, 1961

Women completing at least 80 days of service during the preceding twelve months are entitled to:

- 26 weeks paid maternity leave

### Factories Act & State Shops and Establishments Acts

Regulate:

- Working hours
- Weekly holidays
- Leave
- Overtime
- Employee welfare
