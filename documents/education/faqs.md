# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

## Q1. Who is a workman?

A workman performs manual, technical, clerical, skilled or supervisory work and enjoys statutory protection under labour laws.

---

## Q2. Can an employer terminate without notice?

Generally no. Notice, compensation and legal procedure must be followed depending upon employee category.

---

## Q3. Are post-employment non-compete clauses valid?

Generally no. Indian courts usually hold them void.

---

## Q4. What are legal working hours?

Normally 9 hours/day and 48 hours/week.

---

## Q5. Is WhatsApp evidence admissible?

Yes, subject to Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam requirements.

---

## Q6. How much maternity leave is available?

26 weeks subject to statutory eligibility.
