# REPORTING & GRIEVANCE MECHANISMS

## Internal HR

First point for:

- Salary issues
- Leave disputes
- Harassment

---

## Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

Handles complaints under POSH Act.

---

## Labour Commissioner

Deals with:

- Labour disputes
- Wage issues
- Industrial disputes

---

## Labour Court

Adjudicates disputes involving workmen.

---

## Industrial Tribunal

Handles larger industrial disputes.

---

## High Court

Writ jurisdiction under Article 226.

---

## Supreme Court

Appeals and constitutional remedies.
