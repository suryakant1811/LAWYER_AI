# International Police Cooperation

## Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty (MLAT)

India uses MLAT mechanism for:

- Evidence collection
- Investigation assistance
- Criminal cooperation

Ministry of Home Affairs acts as central authority.

---

## Extradition

Governed by:

Extradition Act, 1962

India has:

- Extradition treaties
- Extradition arrangements

with multiple countries.

---

## INTERPOL

India joined INTERPOL in 1949.

Functions:

- Red Notices
- International investigation support
- Asset tracing

---

## FATF

India became FATF member in 2010.

Focus:

- Anti-money laundering
- Terror financing prevention
