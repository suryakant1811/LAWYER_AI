# Police Law Glossary

## FIR

First Information Report registered for cognisable offences.

## Zero FIR

FIR registered at any police station irrespective of jurisdiction.

## Cognisable Offence

Offence where police can arrest without warrant.

## Non-Cognisable Offence

Police require Magistrate permission.

## BNSS

Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023.

## BNS

Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023.

## Bail

Temporary release of accused pending trial.

## Charge Sheet

Final police report submitted after investigation.
