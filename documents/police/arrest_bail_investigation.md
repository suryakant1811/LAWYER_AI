# Arrest, Bail and Investigation

## Cognisable Offence

Police can:

- Register FIR
- Investigate
- Arrest without warrant

Example:

- Murder
- Theft
- Serious cyber offences

---

## Non-Cognisable Offence

Police require Magistrate permission before investigation.

---

# Bailable Offence

Accused has right to bail.

Bail can be granted by:

- Police officer
- Court

---

# Non-Bailable Offence

Bail is discretionary.

Court decides based on:

- Nature of offence
- Evidence
- Risk factors

---

# Section 55 BNSS — Arrest Without Warrant

Requirements:

- Written order by senior officer
- Details of accused
- Details of offence
- Inform person before arrest

---

# Section 106 BNSS — Property Seizure

Police can seize property connected with crime.

Includes:

- Bank accounts
- Digital assets
- Documents

Magistrate reporting required.
