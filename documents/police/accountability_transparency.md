# Police Accountability and Transparency

## Section 37(b) BNSS

Police stations must maintain information regarding arrested persons.

Designated officer:

- Minimum rank ASI

---

## Section 105 BNSS

Mandatory audio-video recording of:

- Search
- Seizure operations

Recording must be submitted to Magistrate.

---

## Section 176(3) BNSS

Crime scene videography mandatory for offences punishable with 7 years imprisonment or more.

---

## Section 193 BNSS

Police must:

- Inform victim/informant about investigation progress.
- Provide updates within 90 days.

---

## Electronic Evidence

Police reports must include:

- Chain of custody
- Digital evidence handling details
